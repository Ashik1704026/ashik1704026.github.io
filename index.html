About

I design and evaluate ML/QML models for tabular and imaging data in healthcare. Recent work includes comparing classical ML baselines (Logistic Regression, SVM, AdaBoost, CatBoost, XGBoost) against QML methods (QSVM, Variational Quantum Classifier, QNNs) on acute bacterial meningitis (ABM) datasets and microscopy images.

I enjoy reproducible research, clean code, and clear visualizations. I regularly use Colab/Kaggle/Jupyter with scikit‑learn, pandas, qiskit, and pennylane.
